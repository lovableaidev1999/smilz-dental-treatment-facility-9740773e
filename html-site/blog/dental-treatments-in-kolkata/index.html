    1. General & Preventive Dentistry

    Regular dentist visits preserve oral health and help to avoid significant problems.

    Services Involve:

      • Dental Cleanings and Scaling: Clears tartar and plaque.

      • Tooth-Coloured Fillings: Restores cavities while preserving appearance.

      • Fluoride Treatment: Strengthens teeth to stop decay.

      • Gum Disease Treatment: Stops periodontitis and gingivitis.

      • Children’s Dentistry: Particularized treatment for children involving sealants and fluoride application.📍

    📍 Best for: Families looking for a long-term dental care provider in Kolkata.

    2. Cosmetic Dentistry: Perfect Your Smile

    Cosmetic procedures improve your teeth’s looks, thereby increasing your confidence.

    Notable Procedures:

      • Teeth Whitening: Gets stains out so your smile is more brilliant.Some home remedies are here.

      • Smile Design: A multi-disciplinary method to enhance general appearance.

      • Zirconia Laminates (Veneers): Durable and natural-looking.

      • Cosmetic Fillings & Gap Closure: Fixes small flaws for a beautiful appearance.

      • Tooth Stain Removal: Removes discoloration caused by aging, tobacco, or coffee.

    📍 Best for: Individuals seeking a confident, photogenic smile.

    3. Orthodontics—Braces and Aligners

    Orthodontic treatments straighten teeth and enhance bite problems.

    Methods of Treatment:

      • Traditional Braces: Effective for severe misalignment.

      • Ceramic Braces: Less obvious substitutes for metal braces.

      • Invisalign & Clear Aligners: Almost invisible, removable aligners.

      • Retainers: Help preserve tooth alignment following therapy.

    📍 Best for: Teens and adults who want straighter teeth without discomfort.

    4. Restorative Dentistry: Correct & Rebuild Teeth

    Restoring damaged or missing teeth improves function and appearance.

    Typical Techniques:

      • Root Canal Treatment (RCT): Saves an infected tooth from extraction.

      • Dental Crowns & Bridges: Long-lasting materials help restore broken teeth.

      • Full & Partial Dentures: Affordable substitutes for lost teeth.

      • Tooth-Colored or Silver Amalgam Fillings: Help repair cavities.

    📍 Best for: Those suffering from decayed, fractured, or missing teeth.

    Available Treatments Include:

      • Single Tooth Implants: Permanent replacements for a lost tooth.

      • Implant-Supported Dentures: More stable than conventional ones.

      • Full Mouth Rehabilitation: Combining implants, crowns, and bridges to restore all dental function.
      • Implant Supported Dentures: Know details about implant-supported complete dentures, a cost-effective solution for better chewing than ordinary complete dentures..

    📍 Best for: Individuals looking for durable, natural-looking tooth replacement.

    Learn more about Dental Implants at Smilz.

    6. Emergency Dentistry: 24/7 Restitution

    Sudden dental problems like trauma, bleeding, or pain call for immediate treatment.

    Typical Emergency Scenarios:

      • Extreme toothache

      • Knocked-out dental work

      • Fractured or broken tooth

      • Abscesses or swollen gums

      • Post-operative complications

    📍 Best for: Patients experiencing sudden dental discomfort or accidents.
