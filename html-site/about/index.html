    The Clinic

    Modernity with Human Touch

    A Modern Dental Clinic with touch of humanity and 25+ years of experience.

    Located near Garia Andrews College, Kolkata, Smilz has been serving people for more that 25 years with excellence and care in dentistry. Smilz Dental Treatment Facility focuses on the belief of Advance Dentistry at affordable price so that it can reach to everyone who deserve such advanced dental treatments. The journey started in 1999 and is going strong.

    Meet Dr. Dibyendu Dutta

    At Smilz Dental Treatment Facility, your smile is in the hands of one of South Kolkata’s most experienced dental professionals. Since 1999, Dr. Dibyendu Dutta has been a pioneer in patient-centric dental care, combining decades of clinical expertise with a commitment to “Honest Advice and Precise Treatment.”

    Why Patients Trust Dr. Dutta:

    Decades of Experience: With over 27 years in practice, Dr. Dutta has successfully handled thousands of complex cases, from advanced orthodontics to full-mouth rehabilitations.

    Honesty First: Our clinic’s philosophy is built on transparency. Dr. Dutta believes in providing treatments that are necessary and beneficial for the long term, avoiding over-treatment.

    Technological Precision: A firm believer in modern dentistry, he integrates Stent-Guided Implantology and Digital Smile Designing to ensure every procedure is as predictable and painless as possible.

    A Legacy of Care: Located in the heart of Garia, Dr. Dutta has treated generations of families, earning a reputation for a “gentle touch” and a compassionate approach to dental anxiety.

    “My goal is simple: to provide high-quality, affordable dentistry where the patient’s comfort and long-term health always come first.” — Dr. Dibyendu Dutta
